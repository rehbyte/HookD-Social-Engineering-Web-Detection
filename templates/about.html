Detecting deception,
before it hooks you.

HookD analyzes messages, links, and screenshots for the language and signals of social engineering, so a convincing phishing attempt gets a second opinion before you act on it.

How it works

Text analysis

Paste an email or message and HookD scores it for phishing patterns, urgency cues, and sender spoofing.

Image scanning

Upload a screenshot of a suspicious message. On-device OCR extracts the text, then runs the same analysis.

Machine-learning engine

A trained model weighs dozens of linguistic and structural features, combined with a trusted-sender whitelist, to reach a verdict.

Your scan history

Every scan is saved locally to your account so you can revisit verdicts and confidence scores any time.

Reading a verdict

Safe
No strong phishing signals.
Caution
Mixed or uncertain signals.
Unsafe
Strong phishing indicators.
HookD gives probabilistic results based on trained patterns. Treat it as a supplementary check, not a definitive security guarantee. When in doubt, verify the sender through a channel you already trust.
